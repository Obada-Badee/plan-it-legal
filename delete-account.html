Delete Your Plan-it Account

You can permanently delete your Plan-it account and all associated data directly from the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the Plan-it app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings (tap the settings icon on the schedule screen).
  3. Scroll to the Account section and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently and immediately removes:

  • Your account and sign-in credentials
  • All schedules, tasks, and planning history
  • Any notification preferences and app settings tied to your account

This action cannot be undone. No data is retained after deletion.

Can't sign in?

If you're unable to access the app to complete these steps, email obadabadee3@hotmail.com from the address associated with your account and request deletion — we'll delete your account and data manually.
